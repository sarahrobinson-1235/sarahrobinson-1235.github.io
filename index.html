About Me


I wrote my first piece of code 5 years ago in a computer-science course, but ended up taking a different route as a former Store Support Supervisor. I was looking for a career change and was shocked to see how much demand there was and still is for new developers. I have extensive experience in customer service, time management, and multitasking.
